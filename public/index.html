What is a VIN?

The VIN – Vehicle Identification Number – is a unique code and identifier that allows for the identification of a particular vehicle. It’s essential for registration, insurance, and checking history with a VIN decoder.

Where to find a VIN?

The VIN is located on permanent parts of the body, often on the driver’s side dashboard, engine bay, trunk, floor, or passenger door frame depending on the vehicle brand.
